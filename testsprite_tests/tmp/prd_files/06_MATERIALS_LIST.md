# Delivered Materials Tracking

A full list of recurring broad material types that can be delivered on-site. This information is typically extracted from the primary DELIVERY SHEET and serves as a classification guide. 

## DRAINAGE PIPES
- Inner Diameters (ID) typically range from 100mm to 800mm
- Profiles: Wyes (45°), Bends (15°, 30°, 45°, 90°)
- Components: Wyes (AP, ST, VBR) and Couplings (AP, PP, Strabusil)
- End Caps, End Plugs, and Water-tight fixtures
- Profile Sealing Rings (P10, Strabusil) and Reducers

## HV / MV / LV DUCTS & UTILITY PIPING
- Thick-walled Red Pipes (e.g., 160x9.5mm SDR17)
- Substation or distribution protection tubes
- Electrofusion couplers (e.g., EMDS / PE100)
- Structural bends and class LHT directional tubing
- PP Spacers / Block spacings

## PRECAST CONCRETE MANHOLES & CHAMBER RINGS
- Manhole Rings generally span 1200mm to 2400mm widths and 250mm to 1000mm heights
- Heavy precast concrete chambers
- Concrete spacers and sub-structures
- Concrete pipes

## AGGREGATES & LOOSE MATERIALS
- Gravel, Sand, Hardcore Base
- (Requires strict ticket weighting per Delivery Note / Receipt Number / Material Weight in tons)

## PLANT & EQUIPMENT DELIVERIES
- Heavy Machinery: Excavators (8.5t to 30t class), Telehandlers, Rollers, Wheeled excavators
- Attachments: Tilt buckets, Digging buckets, Pointed chisels, Fork extensions
- Site Containers: 20ft / 40ft storage units, fuel tanks, adblue bowsers, site trailers
- Power: Generators, Compressors, pneumatic hammers, electric pumps

## MIXED MATERIALS / FORMWORK / SUNDRIES
- Structural/Safety forms: Plywood sheets (2440x1220x18mm), Formwork Timber various cross-sections
- Barriers: Metal pedestrian barriers, Edge protection panels
- Consumables: Bulk Diesel (Litres), Site gasoline/Alkylate, Additives
- Handheld Equipment: Concrete vibrators, vibratory squeegees, rotary hammers, extension cables
